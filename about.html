What Makes Earnit Special?

Earnit changes the way you interact with your phone. Other habit trackers reward you with phony animations. Other screen time blockers punish you for using your phone. We combine the two using the power of positive reinforcement and reward you with screen time for accomplishing your habits.

New Approach

We decided the best way to manage your screen time is to Earnit. Instead of limiting your screen time, we reward you with it for completing your habits.

Positive Reinforcement

Everyone knows the best way to train a dog is through positive reinforcement. Why would humans be any different?

Not just a fad Diet

Other screen time apps simply limit your screen time. They are fad diets you'll quit after a week. Earnit is a lifestyle change and will help you build better habits, all while using your phone less.
